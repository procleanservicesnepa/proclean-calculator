Window types
Single pane
A single pane window has one fixed or sliding glass panel. Common in older homes, basements, and garages. Each panel counts separately.
One glass panel, interior & exterior.
Double hung
A double hung window has two sashes that slide up and down. Both panels are cleaned on both sides. The most common window type in homes.
Two sashes, both sides cleaned.
Interior ladder required
Windows that require a ladder to access from the interior side — such as high interior windows, stairwell windows, cathedral windows, or skylights accessed from inside. This surcharge applies per window and stacks on top of the window price.

Frequently asked questions
How much does window cleaning cost in Pennsylvania?+
Window cleaning costs vary based on window type, quantity, and condition. Pro Clean Services NEPA has a $200 minimum for residential and commercial jobs and a $25 minimum for storefronts. Use the calculator above to get an instant estimate range for your specific windows.
Why does the calculator show a range instead of an exact price?+
Window cleaning prices can vary based on the actual condition of your windows, accessibility, and other factors we can only assess on-site. The lower end of the range reflects standard conditions, while the upper end accounts for additional complexity. Your final price will be confirmed before any work begins.
Do you charge sales tax on window cleaning?+
Sales tax, when applicable, will be calculated and communicated to you before final booking. We'll make sure everything is clear before any work begins.
What areas does Pro Clean Services NEPA serve?+
Pro Clean Services NEPA is based in Honesdale, PA and serves Pike County, Wayne County, and Lackawanna County in Pennsylvania, as well as Sullivan County and Delaware County in New York. Call us at (570) 470-4330 if you're unsure whether we cover your area.
What is the interior ladder surcharge?+
Some windows — such as those in stairwells, cathedral ceilings, or high interior spaces — require a ladder to access from the inside. Because this adds setup time and complexity, we apply a per-window surcharge on top of the standard window price.
How long does window cleaning take?+
Timing varies depending on the number of windows, their condition, and the complexity of the job. A smaller job may take a couple of hours, while a larger home or commercial property could take a full day. We'll give you a better time estimate when we confirm your appointment.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?+
Not necessarily. If you're booking exterior-only cleaning, you generally do not need to be home. However, if your job includes interior windows or screens, we may need access to remove screens from the inside — so we'll let you know ahead of time. If you'd prefer to remove your own screens beforehand, just mention it when you book.
Do you clean skylights?+
Yes, Pro Clean Services NEPA cleans skylights. Because skylights require ladder access from the interior, the interior ladder surcharge applies in addition to the standard window price. Please mention your skylights when you submit your estimate or give us a call and we'll factor them into your quote.
Do you clean storm windows or Pella windows?+
Yes, Pro Clean Services NEPA cleans storm windows and Pella windows. These specialty systems may carry an additional fee because cleaning them properly often requires disassembling the window — removing the sashes, tracks, and interior components — which adds significant labor compared to a standard window. We'll always review this with you before your appointment so there are no surprises on pricing.
Do you clean storefront windows?+
Yes! Pro Clean Services NEPA cleans storefront glass for retail shops, restaurants, offices, and other commercial businesses across NEPA and the NY Catskills. Our storefront minimum is $25.
Do you clean windows with hard water stains or paint?+
Yes. Pro Clean Services NEPA can treat windows with hard water deposits, paint overspray, oxidation, and construction residue. These require additional time and product so an extra fee may apply. We always discuss charges before starting — no surprises.
Is there a minimum charge for window cleaning?+
Yes. Pro Clean Services NEPA has a $200 minimum for residential and commercial window cleaning per job, and a $25 minimum for storefront window cleaning. The calculator automatically adjusts your estimate to reflect the minimum if needed.
